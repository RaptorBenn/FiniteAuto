Project goals

The aim of this site is to provide resources relevent to the automotive recycling trade, from the start it will be divided into four pages, the Homepage, Resources (Gallery), The Model List, and the About/Contact page.

For those who don't work in the industry, yours isn't the same as every other model of the same year, alot of parts change mid year and others can be dependent on the country of origin and a variety of other factors. And I cannot stress enough the fact that they are hardly ever all the same.

This website is a personal project of Benn Clarke, all files and documents are my own intellectual property and are not available for public or private use without explicit permission from myself (the owner).

Resources

The resources section is planned to contain diagrams, and general information regarding vehicle specific changes to running models, part compatibility and fitting advice, and other relevant materials.

Model List

The model list is still in the design stage, the aim is to provide a tool for finding every permutation and variation of any make of vehicle.
This is quite a large problem as this information is simply not available and has to be either bought or gleaned from secondary sources.
